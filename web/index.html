Game Description

Discover the ultimate test of your estimation skills with Dawaze! Dawaze is an exciting new game where maps and guessing games collide. In this captivating game, you will be presented with two points on a map, along with the route connecting them and the time & distance it takes to drive between them.

Your challenge? Click on two other points on the map that you believe have a similar driving duration between them as the original route. Can you accurately gauge the driving durations and find the perfect matches? The clock is ticking, and precision is key!

Dawaze offers a strategic game experience that combines strategy, logic, and a dash of luck. Sharpen your observation skills, route analysis, and trust your instincts to make the right choices. With each successful match, you'll earn points over a series of ten rounds, challenging you to push your boundaries further and get on the leaderboard.

Whether you're a travel enthusiast, geography buff, or simply love a good guessing game, Dawaze promises endless excitement and entertainment. So, can you conquer the roads and become the ultimate Dawazer? Play now and find out!
